About Us

Only Tuition Centre in
Patturaikkal Covering All Subjects

Including Social Science — taught by subject professionals who truly care.

Excel Tuition Centre offers personalized learning in small, focused groups of 15–20 students per batch, for classes 7th to 12th standard — both CBSE and ICSE. Experienced subject professionals deliver interactive classes, ensuring every student gets the individual attention they need to thrive.

Along with offline school tuitions, our faculty also provides online school tuitions and home tuitions for students. Our priority of giving individual attention allows us to identify each student's strengths and weak areas, guiding them to excel in their academic exams.

We are a team of teachers with more than 15 years of experience in education, united by a passion for teaching and a shared dream — to create a tuition centre where every child is nurtured with proper, concept-based learning.

Meet Our Founder

The Heart Behind
Excel

Uma Venkitesh
Founder & Lead Educator

Uma Venkitesh founded Excel Tuition Centre with a deep passion for education and a drive to make quality learning accessible to every student. Holding a B.Tech in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from NSS College of Engineering, Palakkad, and an MCA from IGNOU, she began her teaching career in 2014.

With experience at Chinmaya Mission College, Chelakkara Central School (CBSE), and Harisree Vidya Nidhi School (ICSE), Uma brings a wealth of institutional knowledge and a genuine love for nurturing students — one concept at a time.
